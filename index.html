Auto Bot (server): After Start, the first scan runs in ~10s, then every BOT_CHECK_INTERVAL_MIN minutes (default 15). Each cycle loads portfolio, leaderboard activity, and many markets; scores them (volume, liquidity, implied odds, top-trader alignment, leaderboard PnL context); respects daily limit, max % per trade, max exposure, and min score; then places at most one buy if a setup clears the bar. Ensure PRIVATE_KEY and funded USDC — otherwise orders may stay simulated/pending.
